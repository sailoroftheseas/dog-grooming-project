Our mission at doggrooming.com is to offer clients a personal and pleasurable grooming experience for their dogs in a safe and enjoyable atmosphere, without compromise. We use only the finest natural shampoos and conditioners and never cage dry pets. Our method is significantly more intimate and time consuming than the typical grooming process, but the end result is a happy pet that has been cared for the way you would yourself: with love.
